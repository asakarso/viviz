VIVIZ

VIVIZ (Hangul: 비비지; RR: Bibiji) is a South Korean girl group formed by BPM Entertainment, consists of former GFRIEND members: Eunha, SinB, and Umji. The group debuted on February 9, 2022, with their first mini album 'Beam of Prism'. The group has given their fandom an official name called Na.V (pronounced 'Na-Vi) on January 21, 2022. The fandom name resembling the Korean word for butterfly, 'Na-bi'. The name combines the word 'Na', which represents 'me' in Korean, with the first character of VIVIZ, 'Vi'.
